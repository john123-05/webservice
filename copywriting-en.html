The problem

Your website looks good, but...

...Visitors leave without doing anything. No call. No request. No reaction.

It's rarely the design. Mostly it's because of the lyrics. Too general. Too technical. Too focused on the company – and not enough on the customer.

What most websites say

  • “We have been in the industry for 20 years”
  • “Quality and reliability are our top priority”
  • “Feel free to talk to us”
  • “We offer tailor-made solutions”

What visitors want to hear

  • What are you solving for me – specifically?
  • Why should I trust you?
  • What happens if I request?
  • Why you and not the competition?

What does that cost?

Bad texts cost you customers every day

Every visitor who leaves your site without asking is a potential customer who goes to the competition. Not because your offer is worse - but because their website communicates more clearly.

Unclear message

Visitors don't understand what you're offering in the first few seconds - and they drop out.

No trust

Without the right words, your company will seem interchangeable. Price becomes the only decision criterion.

No next step

If no one knows what to do next, they don't do anything. There are no inquiries.

Our solution

Texts that turn visitors into customers

Good copywriting doesn't mean writing flowery sentences. It means finding the right words – at the right time, for the right people.

clarity

Visitors immediately understand what you offer and who it is intended for.

Trust

By using the right wording you can build credibility – before anyone asks.

Conversion

Clear calls to action guide visitors specifically to inquiries.

Result

More qualified inquiries – from visitors who are already convinced before they write.

Lead funnels

Not everyone is ready to ask straight away – and that’s okay

Many visitors are interested in your offer, but are not yet ready to contact you directly. A lead funnel picks you up exactly there – with the right texts, at the right time.

Whether it's a short quiz funnel, free PDF or email sequence: the text is the heart. He decides whether someone clicks, responds – or jumps off.

Quiz funnel

Visitors answer a few questions and get something valuable in return - you get a qualified lead.

Lead magnet

A free PDF, checklist or mini-guide with strong texts that build trust and lead to the request.

Email sequence

After the first contact, you will automatically stay top of mind with a series of targeted emails.

Landing page

A focused page with a clear message and a single goal: the request.

This makes your website a tool for more inquiries

A good website doesn't just provide information - it leads visitors to the next one step.

Case Study · Lift pictures

How Liftpictures internationally generates more inquiries via the website

Liftpictures offers photo and video systems for theme parks and attractions worldwide.

The original website was primarily aimed at the German market and had no clear path to lead generation.

1 · New website for international visitors

The website has been restructured so that international target groups can Understand the offer more quickly and make contact more easily.

2 · Lead funnel for lead generation

In addition, a short quiz funnel was integrated. Visitors answer some Questions and then receive a suitable PDF by email.

This means that visitors who do not yet want to inquire directly can become leads be recorded.

Result

  • international visibility improved
  • additional lead channel via quiz funnel
  • structured path from visitor → inquiry
