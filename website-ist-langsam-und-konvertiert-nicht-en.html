If your website is slow and still not convincing

Performance and requests belong together

Slow websites not only cost rankings, but also trust and inquiries. Especially on mobile devices, a large proportion of visitors drop out if pages take too long to load or appear restless.

  • Poor mobile loading times
  • Too big media and unnecessary load
  • Weak user guidance despite existing content
  • Little trust and no clear trigger for action

What mostly needs to be improved here

Often two problems come together at the same time: a technical weakness and a weak conversion lead in terms of content. Then even existing traffic does not bring enough business results.

This is particularly critical for local companies because many interested parties search mobile, quickly and with a clear intention to take action.

Is loading time really that important?

Yes. Loading time influences user behavior, mobile usability and also the search engine perception of a website.

Is image compression alone enough?

Not always. It is often about technical structure, unnecessary load, interaction logic and the entire site architecture.

Why is this a SEO topic?

Because technical quality, user signals and website experience are closely related to visibility and conversion.
