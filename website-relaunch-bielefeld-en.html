Frequently asked questions

Does everything have to be completely redone during the relaunch?

Not always. Good content or elements can often be adopted. What is crucial is that the structure, message and technology are consistent in the end.

Is a relaunch bad for SEO?

Only if it is implemented improperly. With a clean page structure, canonicals and internal logic, a relaunch can significantly improve SEO.

When should you optimize rather than relaunch?

If the website is fundamentally viable and only needs targeted content or technical improvements.
