The implementation of a new website usually takes between 2 and 6 weeks, depending the scope of the project and the availability of the content. Smaller websites can can be implemented more quickly, while larger projects with multiple pages or special ones functions require a little more time.

Do I have to provide text and images myself?

Not necessarily. A lot of content can be developed or optimized together. If If texts, images or logos already exist, these can of course be used. If content is missing, we support you in providing clear and understandable content for them Develop website.

Will my website also work well on smartphones?

Yes. The majority of visitors today come to websites via smartphones. Therefore will all websites built to work just as well on mobile as they do on Desktop devices.

Can my website also be found better on Google?

Can I edit my website myself later?

Yes. Most websites are built with a system that allows content to update or expand yourself later. This way you remain flexible and can Make changes at any time.
