Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between web design and website relaunch?

Web design describes the strategic and visual design of a website. A relaunch usually means the revision of an existing site including structure, content and technology.

Is web design alone enough for good rankings?

No. Good web design supports SEO, but does not replace clear information architecture, clean Meta data and relevant content.

For which industries is web design particularly worthwhile?

Especially for local service providers, craft businesses, consultancies, practices and companies where trust and clarity lead directly to inquiries.
