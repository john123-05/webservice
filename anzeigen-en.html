Organic vs. Paid

SEO is long-term. Ads work immediately.

SEO will get you to the top permanently - but it takes time. If you need inquiries now, Paid ads are the fastest way.

The difference to SEO: You pay per click. You will be visible within hours – for exactly the people who are currently looking for your service or who belong to your target group.

The two channels

Google Ads or Meta Ads – which one suits you?

Both work. But they speak to people in different moments.

Google Ads

Active search

Someone is actively looking for what you have to offer. Your ad will appear exactly then – at the top of Google.

  • High purchase intent – ​​the user is already searching
  • Perfect for services with clear demand
  • Measurable down to the last cent
  • Immediate visibility, even without SEO ranking

Best for: Trades, service providers, local services

Meta Ads

Address target groups

Your target group is scrolling through Instagram or Facebook - and sees your ad before they even know they need what you offer.

  • Precise targeting by location, age, interests
  • Ideal for offers that you are not actively looking for
  • Strong visual impact via image & video
  • Cheaper reach than Google with a broader target group

Best for: Gastronomy, products, events, awareness

The problem

Ads are easy to set up – and expensive if you do it wrong

Google and Meta earn money from every click. They want you to spend money – not necessarily get results. Without experience in targeting, ad design and tracking, you will quickly burn through budget without any measurable impact.

Incorrect targeting

Your ad will be shown to people who would never buy - your budget is gone before a real customer clicks.

No conversion tracking

Without proper tracking, you don't know which ad is bringing in inquiries - and you continue to pay blindly.

Weak ad copy

Who doesn't click if the ad is generic. Without strong copy, the budget is wasted on the way to the first click.
