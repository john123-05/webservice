Frequently asked questions

How quickly do you see results with SEO?

That depends on competition, initial situation and search intention. Improvements in structure, visibility and appropriate rankings often appear first before they result in a stable increase in inquiries.

Is SEO only useful for large companies?

No. Local companies in particular benefit from clean SEO because they usually want to be visible in clearly defined markets.

Do you need new content regularly?

Not necessarily in large numbers. It is often more effective to clearly structure the most important performance and problem pages and to link them well internally.
