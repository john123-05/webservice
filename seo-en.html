Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between SEO and Google Ads?

With Google Ads you buy the first place on Google - this means ongoing costs and payment per click. SEO takes a little longer (approx. 90 days), but is permanently free after that and usually delivers significantly better results. The best results come from a combination of both approaches - however, the SEO foundation should already be in place before you start with Google ads or similar.

Which companies do you work with?

We mainly work with local service providers and craft businesses in Bielefeld and OWL - companies that want to be found via Google maps and need specific inquiries instead of just clicks.

How much time do I have to invest myself?

Very little. After onboarding, we take care of most of the work. All you have to do is provide one-time access to your Google profile - we'll do the rest.
