Frequently asked questions

Do craft businesses really need more than a simple website?

Often yes. Especially in local competition, clear services, signals of trust, clean mobile use and a simple contact channel are what count.

Does there have to be a lot of references from the craft industry?

Not necessarily. What is more important is that the website reflects the typical decision-making factors of craft customers well.

Does a better website also help with Google?

Yes. If services, regions and contact channels are presented more clearly, this helps both users and search engines.
