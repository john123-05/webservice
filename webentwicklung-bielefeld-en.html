Frequently asked questions

How is web development different from web design?

Web design focuses more on structure, message and presentation. Web development focuses on technical implementation, performance and expandability.

Is web development also relevant for smaller websites?

Yes, as soon as performance, maintainability or individual requirements become important.

Does good web development also help with SEO?

Yes. Clean technology, fast loading times and good page structure help search engines crawl and understand content better.
